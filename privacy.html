Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1. No Data Collection

Modulist is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. Our app operates entirely on your device without requiring accounts or data transmission.

2. No Account Required

Modulist does not require user accounts or registration. You can use the app immediately without providing any personal information such as names, email addresses, or other identifying data.

3. Local Data Only

All your activity lists and planning data are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your lists, preferences, or any other information you create within the app.

4. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use analytics tools, tracking cookies, or any other methods to monitor user behavior. Your app usage remains completely private and unknown to us.

5. Third-Party Services

Modulist does not integrate with third-party services that would collect your data. The app functions independently without external data processing.

6. Contact Information

If you choose to contact us for support or feedback, we will only use the information you provide (such as your email address) to respond to your inquiry. We do not store or use this information for any other purpose.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
