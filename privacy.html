Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

1. Data Collection and Storage

What We Collect: Repurplist is designed with your privacy as our top priority. We do not collect, store, or process any of your personal information on our servers. All your data, including checklists and activities, is stored exclusively on your device and securely synced with your private iCloud account.

How We Collect: Since we do not collect any data, there are no collection methods to describe.

How We Use Data: We do not use any user data as we do not collect any.

2. Third-Party Data Sharing

Repurplist does not share any user data with third parties because we do not collect any data. We do not use analytics tools, advertising networks, third-party SDKs, or any other services that would have access to user data.

If this policy changes in the future, any third party with whom we share user data will be required to provide the same or equal protection of user data as stated in this privacy policy.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

We do not store your data on our servers, so there is nothing for us to retain or delete. All your activity lists and planning data are stored on your device and in your private iCloud account. You have full control and can delete your data at any time by removing it from within the app or by uninstalling the app.

If you have contacted us for support and provided your email address, you can request deletion of any correspondence by emailing us at protonbleu@gmail.com.

4. User Consent and Permissions

Repurplist does not require any special permissions or user consent for data collection since we do not collect any data. The app functions entirely offline and does not request access to photos, contacts, location, or any other sensitive data.

If you choose to contact us for support, we will only use the information you provide (such as your email address) to respond to your inquiry. We do not store or use this information for any other purpose.

5. No Account Required

Repurplist does not require user accounts or registration. You can use the app immediately without providing any personal information such as names, email addresses, or other identifying data.

6. Local Data Only

All your activity lists and planning data are stored locally on your device and synced with your private iCloud account. We do not have access to your lists, preferences, or any other information you create within the app.

7. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use analytics tools, tracking cookies, or any other methods to monitor user behavior. Your app usage remains completely private and unknown to us.

8. Third-Party Services

Repurplist does not integrate with third-party services that would collect your data. The app functions independently without external data processing.

9. Children's Privacy

Repurplist is not specifically designed for children under 13, but it can be used by people of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or would like to exercise any of your privacy rights, please contact us at:

Email: protonbleu@gmail.com
